Turkey Sandwich


Ingredients:

2 slices (1/2 inch thick) hearty country bread

4 slices roasted turkey breast

1 slice pepperjack cheese

2 teaspoons butter

4 teaspoons strawberry preserves


Recipe:

Heat skillet over medium heat.

Butter one side of each bread slice.

Place one slice in skillet.

Top with turkey and cheese.

Place second slice on top, butter side up.

Cook until golden brown, 3-5 minutes.

Caprese Salad

Caprese Salad


Ingredients:

4 large ripe tomatoes, sliced

1 fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced

Fresh basil leaves

2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar

Salt and pepper to taste


Recipe:

1. Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices on a plate, alternating them.

2. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the slices.

3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.

4. Season with salt and pepper.

5. Serve immediately.

Chicken Caesar Wrap


Ingredients:

1 large flour tortilla

1 cup cooked chicken, shredded

1 cup romaine lettuce, chopped

1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

2 tablespoons Caesar dressing

1/4 cup croutons


Recipe:

1. Lay the tortilla flat and spread Caesar dressing evenly.

2. Layer chicken, lettuce, Parmesan cheese, and croutons in the center.

3. Fold the sides of the tortilla inward and roll tightly.

4. Slice in half and serve.

Vegetable Stir-Fry


Ingredients:

2 cups broccoli florets

1 red bell pepper, sliced

1 yellow bell pepper, sliced

1 cup snap peas

1 carrot, thinly sliced

2 tablespoons soy sauce

1 tablespoon sesame oil

1 clove garlic, minced


Recipe:

1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.

2. Add garlic and sauté for 30 seconds.

3. Add broccoli, bell peppers, snap peas, and carrots. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.

4. Pour in soy sauce and toss to coat.

5. Serve hot with rice or noodles.

Beef Tacos


Ingredients:

1 lb ground beef

1 packet taco seasoning

8 small taco shells

1 cup shredded lettuce

1 cup diced tomatoes

1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

1/4 cup sour cream


Recipe:

1. Cook ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until browned.

2. Add taco seasoning and water as per packet instructions. Simmer for 5 minutes.

3. Warm taco shells in the oven or microwave.

4. Fill each shell with beef, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and a dollop of sour cream.

5. Serve immediately.

Pasta Primavera

$10.99

Pasta Primavera


Ingredients:

8 oz pasta (penne or fusilli)

1 cup broccoli florets

1 cup sliced zucchini

1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

2 tablespoons olive oil

2 cloves garlic, minced

Salt and pepper to taste


Recipe:

1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté for 1 minute.

3. Add broccoli, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes. Cook for 5-7 minutes until tender.

4. Toss the cooked pasta with the vegetables.

5. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Serve warm.

Greek Salad

Greek Salad


Ingredients:

2 cups chopped romaine lettuce

1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

1 cucumber, sliced

1/2 red onion, thinly sliced

1/2 cup Kalamata olives

1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 tablespoon red wine vinegar

1 teaspoon dried oregano


Recipe:

1. In a large bowl, combine lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and olives.

2. Sprinkle feta cheese on top.

3. Drizzle with olive oil and red wine vinegar.

4. Sprinkle with oregano and toss gently.

5. Serve immediately.
