Classic Pancakes


Ingredients:

1 cup all-purpose flour

2 tablespoons sugar

1 tablespoon baking powder

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 cup milk

1 egg

2 tablespoons melted butter


Recipe:

1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.

2. Add milk, egg, and melted butter. Stir until combined.

3. Heat a skillet over medium heat and lightly grease.

4. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.

5. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden brown.

Cheese Omelette


Ingredients:

3 eggs

1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese

1 tablespoon butter

Salt and pepper to taste


Recipe:

1. Beat eggs in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.

2. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat.

3. Pour eggs into the skillet and let them set slightly.

4. Sprinkle cheese on one half of the omelette.

5. Fold the omelette in half and cook until cheese melts.

Avocado Toast


Ingredients:

1 ripe avocado

2 slices whole-grain bread

1 tablespoon olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

Optional: red pepper flakes or poached egg


Recipe:

1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.

2. Mash the avocado in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.

3. Spread the mashed avocado evenly on the toast.

4. Drizzle with olive oil and add optional toppings.

5. Serve immediately.

Berry Smoothie Bowl


Ingredients:

1 frozen banana

1/2 cup frozen mixed berries

1/2 cup almond milk

Toppings: granola, fresh berries, chia seeds


Recipe:

1. Blend banana, berries, and almond milk until smooth.

2. Pour into a bowl.

3. Top with granola, fresh berries, and chia seeds.

4. Serve immediately.

French Toast


Ingredients:

4 slices bread

2 eggs

1/2 cup milk

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Butter for frying


Recipe:

1. Whisk eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon in a bowl.

2. Dip bread slices into the mixture, coating both sides.

3. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat.

4. Cook bread until golden brown on both sides.

5. Serve with syrup or fresh fruit.

Egg Sandwich


Ingredients:

Recipe:

2. Cook egg sunny-side up
