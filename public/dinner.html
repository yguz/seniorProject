Grilled Salmon


Ingredients:

2 salmon fillets

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 teaspoon garlic powder

1 teaspoon paprika

Salt and pepper to taste

1 lemon, sliced


Recipe:

1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.

2. Brush salmon with olive oil and season with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.

3. Place salmon on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes per side.

4. Serve with lemon slices.

Beef Stew


Ingredients:

1 lb beef stew meat

2 cups beef broth

1 cup carrots, sliced

1 cup potatoes, diced

1 onion, chopped

2 tablespoons flour

2 tablespoons olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste


Recipe:

1. Heat olive oil in a large pot and brown the beef.

2. Add onions and cook until softened.

3. Sprinkle flour over the beef and stir to coat.

4. Add beef broth, carrots, and potatoes. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 1.5 hours.

5. Season with salt and pepper before serving.

Chicken Alfredo


Ingredients:

8 oz fettuccine pasta

2 chicken breasts, sliced

1 cup heavy cream

1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

2 tablespoons butter

2 cloves garlic, minced

Salt and pepper to taste


Recipe:

1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.

2. In a skillet, cook chicken in butter until browned. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.

3. Pour in heavy cream and Parmesan cheese. Stir until thickened.

4. Toss pasta in the sauce and serve.

Vegetable Lasagna


Ingredients:

9 lasagna noodles

2 cups marinara sauce

1 cup ricotta cheese

1 cup spinach, chopped

1 cup zucchini, sliced

1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded

1/4 cup Parmesan cheese


Recipe:

1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

2. Layer noodles, marinara sauce, ricotta, spinach, zucchini, and cheeses in a baking dish.

3. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella and Parmesan.

4. Bake for 30 minutes or until bubbly.

Shrimp Scampi

$13.99

Shrimp Scampi


Ingredients:

1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined

8 oz linguine pasta

4 tablespoons butter

4 cloves garlic, minced

1/2 cup white wine

1/4 cup parsley, chopped

Salt and pepper to taste


Recipe:

1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.

2. In a skillet, melt butter and sauté garlic until fragrant.

3. Add shrimp and cook until pink. Pour in white wine and simmer for 2 minutes.

4. Toss pasta in the sauce, garnish with parsley, and serve.

Stuffed Peppers


Ingredients:

4 bell peppers, tops cut off and seeds removed

1 lb ground beef

1 cup cooked rice

1 cup marinara sauce

1/2 cup shredded cheese

1 onion, chopped

Salt and pepper to taste


Recipe:

1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

2. Cook ground beef and onions in a skillet. Mix with rice and marinara sauce.

3. Stuff peppers with the mixture and top with cheese.

4. Bake for 25-30 minutes.

$14.99

Roast Chicken


Ingredients:

1 whole chicken

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 lemon, halved

4 cloves garlic

1 tablespoon rosemary

Salt and pepper to taste


Recipe:

1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).

2. Rub chicken with olive oil, salt, pepper, and rosemary.

3. Stuff cavity with lemon and garlic.

4. Roast for 1.5 hours or until internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).

Lamb Chops

$16.99

Lamb Chops


Ingredients:

4 lamb chops

2 tablespoons olive oil

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 tablespoon rosemary

Salt and pepper to taste


Recipe:

1. Season lamb chops with salt, pepper, garlic, and rosemary.

2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.

3. Sear lamb chops for 3-4 minutes per side for medium-rare.

4. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
